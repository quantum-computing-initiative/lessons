The Oracle

I give you a black box with a function in it.

You cannot see inside, but you can put bits through it and see the output.

How many queries on a classical computer does it take to determine which of the 4 functions it is? (answer: 2)

How about on a quantum computer? (answer: also 2 -- there are 4 possible states, so we need at minimum 2 bits of information)

How many queries does it take on a classical computer to find if it's variable/constant? (answer: 2 -- go through the queries)

How about on a quantum computer? (answer: 1 -- this is our first example of quantum outperforming classical)
